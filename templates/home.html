How It Works

Applicants submit their personal, financial and property details through a simple form. The trained model instantly evaluates the application and predicts whether the loan is likely to be approved or rejected, allowing credit officers to fast-track low-risk cases and flag high-risk ones for further review.

📊 Data-Driven Decisions
